Inha Amateur Astronomical Association

Get Started

IAAA recruits new members who are interested in stars.

Stars and Planets are familiar to us, but there are not many opportunities to see it. We observes the celestial objects that were only seen in photos and videos with a telescope in the club. Every Wednesday, seminars are held to supplement the knowledge required for observation. In addition, We share how to capture the stars using the camera.

IAAA's Observation

We keep regular observations once a semester,considering the date. On a clear day, small astronomical observations are held on an irregular basis. You can also learn about deep sky and how to use the telescope through observation trips during vacation.

Public Observation

We hold an event to show the moon to people at the University Square, Incheon Park.

Photo Exhibition

Star-photo exhibitions are held based on astronomical photographs taken by members of IAAA during one semester.
